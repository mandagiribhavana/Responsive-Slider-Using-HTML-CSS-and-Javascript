MatiraBeach

Matira Beach is renowned for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and idyllic setting. Stretching over a mile, this public beach is ideal for swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing. Its shallow, warm waters are perfect for wading and observing vibrant marine life, including colorful fish and graceful sea turtles.

MyrtosBeach

Myrtos Beach, located on the island of Kefalonia in Greece. Renowned for its striking beauty, Myrtos Beach features a crescent-shaped coastline bordered by steep cliffs and lush vegetation. The clear turquoise waters and pristine white pebbles make it a popular spot for swimming and sunbathing.

NapiliBay

Napili Bay is a picturesque beach located on the island of Maui in Hawaii. Known for its clear, calm waters and beautiful sandy shore, it's a popular spot for swimming, snorkeling, and relaxing under the sun.
